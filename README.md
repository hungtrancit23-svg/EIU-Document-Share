# EIU Archive - Website sharing learning documents.

Project introduction:
The project is implemented by group 4 - CSW 303.
Our platform assists EIU students in finding and downloading essential documents for next quarters, besides they also may upload their documents for others. To ensure high-quality content, all uploaded documents must be reviewed and approved by the administrators.
